Roman Numerals
by Craig Froehle
froehlcm@ucbeh.san.uc.edu

I	1
V	5
X	10
L	50
C	100
D	500
M	1000
Note: Placing a bar above any of the numerals (larger than I) multiplies 
that numeral times 1,000

Examples:
IV = 4
VII = 7
IX = 9
XI = 11 
XIX = 19
XL = 40
LX = 60  
CL = 150
CXC = 190 
CC = 200
CCC = 300
CD = 400
MCMXCIV = 1994
